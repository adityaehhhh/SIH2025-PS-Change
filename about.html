About Oil-Edge

Who We Are

Oil-Edge is an innovative platform developed by Team CrackO’Nerd to revolutionize the oilseed by-product value chain. Our core goal is to empower farmers and processors by leveraging IoT, Blockchain, and AI to capture the true value of high-potential by-products like soymeal, mustard cake, and groundnut cake, addressing inefficiencies in India's agriculture sector.

Using advanced features like blockchain traceability and AI-powered market predictions, we ensure transparent, efficient supply chains. Oil-Edge helps boost farmer incomes by reducing wastage, enabling premium pricing, and providing real-time insights, ultimately supporting food security and rural development.

Our Vision

We envision a future where oilseed farmers capture fair value from every part of their produce, transforming fragmented chains into transparent, tech-driven ecosystems. Our platform is designed to democratize access to markets, quality verification, and incentives, fostering sustainable agriculture and aligning with India's Digital Agriculture Mission for equitable growth.

Why Oil-Edge?

  • Transparent Traceability: Blockchain-powered "Oil Passport" for verifiable origin and quality, unlocking premium markets and exports.
  • AI-Driven Insights: Predictive pricing, demand forecasting, and quality recommendations to navigate market volatility effectively.
  • Inclusive Accessibility: Multilingual chatbot in 50+ languages with voice and WhatsApp support, bridging digital divides for rural users.
  • Incentive System: Earn "Oilcoins" for high-quality produce and redeem for agri-inputs, encouraging best practices and sustainability.
  • Unified Marketplace: Secure payments, order tracking, and logistics integration to streamline transactions and reduce intermediary costs.

Our Technology Stack

  • React & Flutter 📱: For responsive web and mobile interfaces ensuring seamless user experience.
  • Python & FastAPI 🐍: Backend for robust API services and data processing.
  • TensorFlow & PyTorch 🤖: AI/ML models for price forecasting and quality prediction.
  • Hyperledger Fabric ⛓️: Blockchain for immutable traceability and smart contracts.
  • Dialogflow & Twilio 💬: For multilingual chatbot and WhatsApp integration.
